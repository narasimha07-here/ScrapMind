=== Hostel Fee Structure (AY 2025-26) — VIT-AP University ===

=== OVERVIEW ===
VIT-AP University provides comfortable and secure hostel facilities for students with options for AC/Non-AC rooms, shared apartments, and dormitories. The annual hostel fee includes Room, Mess, and Laundry services across different accommodation types and meal plans.

=== SALIENT_FEATURES ===
- Comprehensive fee structure covering room, mess, and laundry
- Multiple accommodation options: 2-bed to 20-bed configurations
- AC and Non-AC room variants available
- Three mess options: Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, and Special
- Separate pricing for Indian, NRI/Foreign, and Research Scholar categories
- Apartment-style accommodation available for select configurations
