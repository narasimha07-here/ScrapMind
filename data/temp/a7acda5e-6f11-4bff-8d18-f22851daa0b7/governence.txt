=== Governance Structure — VIT-AP University ===

=== OVERVIEW ===
VIT-AP University is guided by a multi-tiered governance model with leadership from the VIT group. Key decision bodies include the Governing Body, Board of Management, Academic Council, Finance Committee, Planning and Monitoring Board, and Research Advisory Committee. These bodies ensure high standards in administration, academics, financial management, planning, and research.
