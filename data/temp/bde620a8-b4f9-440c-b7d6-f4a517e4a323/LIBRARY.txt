=== Library — VIT-AP University ===

=== OVERVIEW ===
The VIT-AP University Library is a technology-driven, user-centric, innovative platform for the academic community with the motto "Apply Knowledge and Improve Life®." It maintains a balance between traditional and modern concepts of library service, equipped with modern facilities and resources including digital collections, databases, and comprehensive academic materials across all disciplines.

=== SALIENT_FEATURES ===
- Technology-driven and user-centric approach
- Balance between traditional and modern library concepts
- Comprehensive collections in engineering, basic sciences, humanities, management, law
- Digital library with modern computing facilities
- Centrally air-conditioned and Wi-Fi enabled environment
- CCTV surveillance for security and safety
- Multiple specialized areas for different academic needs

=== LIBRARY_RESOURCES ===
Print_Collections:
